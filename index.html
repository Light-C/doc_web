About DocuSay

Nowadays, many companies work with paper-based compliance documents, which are prone to errors, poor readability, damage, and destruction, and some industry participants have failed completely to complete proper compliance documentation.

The failure of mobile workers to complete and manage documents on the go is costing the Industry millions per week and trillions of dollars per year. The costs include financial losses, legal losses, time losses, security breaches, etc.

Our project aims to develop a virtual assistant for mobile workers, which is a voice-based form completion for document and automation management on the go, that is Docsay.

Cross-platform

DocuSay supports cross-platform use. Whether it's an Android phone or an Apple phone or a computer, our app is supported for use.
